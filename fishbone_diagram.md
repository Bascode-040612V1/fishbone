## Summary
This Fishbone diagram identifies the main problem of communication barriers between hearing-impaired individuals and the general public, and categorizes the contributing causes into six major categories:
1. Methods/Methodology
2. Materials/Resources
3. People/Participants
4. Equipment/Technology
5. Environment/Context
6. Processes/Procedures

Each category branches out into specific causes that contribute to the main problem, helping to visualize the complex interrelationships between various factors affecting the communication challenges faced by the hearing-impaired community in the Philippines.
